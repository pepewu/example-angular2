What is Flaneur?

Flaneur is a simple tool that will help you to plan your trips. It can help you to

  • manage and collect places you want to visit
  • create very simple orvery detailed trips plans according to your needs
  • manage trips budget and schedule

Who is it for?

Flaneur may be useful for you if only:

  • you love big cities - exploring them, strolling, discovering places
  • you like to plan your trip carefully - planning is almost as pleasurable as trip itself
  • you enyoy to collect places to visit during the trip, store them on map and describe them
  • you are on a low budget, so every amount of money should be spent carefully

How to use it?

  1. Add city in 'Places' library
  2. Add places to that city
  3. Create trip
  4. Add and setup trip chapters
